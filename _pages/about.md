I am a computational neuroscientist, data scientist and machine learning engineer with experience in clinical data analysis, biomarker development, statistical modelling and developing machine learning solutions. As a Scientific Advisor at Innodem Neurosciences with over 3 years of experience, I contribute expertise in biomarker development, machine learning, and data analysis to support the assessment of neurological disorders. My role involves analyzing and interpreting real-world digital health data, developing biomarkers, and delivering evidence-based insights to inform product strategy and regulatory-compliant decisions. 

I am currently completing a PhD in Neuroscience at McGill University in the lab of [Dr. Anmar Khadra](https://www.medicine.mcgill.ca/physio/khadralab/)  with over 10 years of research experience, my work integrates computational and data-driven approaches to explore neuronal properties and circuit dynamics. My mission is to bridge advanced data science methodologies with neuroscience to uncover mechanistic insights and enhance neurological health.

I completed my Master of Science in Neural and Behavioural Science from the Eberhard Karls Universität Tübingen in Tübingen, Germany where I worked in the lab of [Dr. Jan Benda](https://uni-tuebingen.de/fakultaeten/mathematisch-naturwissenschaftliche-fakultaet/fachbereiche/biologie/institute/neurobiologie/lehrbereiche/neuroethologie/) in collaboration with [Dr. Holger Lerche](https://www.hih-tuebingen.de/forschung/neurologie-mit-schwerpunkt-epileptologie/), [Dr. Yuanyuan Liu](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Yuanyuan-Liu-66), [Dr. Ulrike Hedrich-Klimosch](https://www.hih-tuebingen.de/forschung/neurologie-mit-schwerpunkt-epileptologie/forschungsgruppen/jfg-hedrich-klimosch) and Dr. Stephan Lauxmann on computational modelling of ion channel mutations associated with epilepsy and episodic ataxis, personalized medicine approaches for channelopathies, and *in silico* screening of therapeutic approaches.
